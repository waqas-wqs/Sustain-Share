A Move for World Hunger

Sustain Share is dedicated to creating sustainable solutions to combat world hunger by leveraging the power of communities and industries worldwide.

Fighting Hunger, One Move at a Time

Hunger is a global crisis affecting millions of people across the world. From impoverished communities to developed nations, the struggle to access sufficient, nutritious food is a daily reality for far too many. To combat this pressing issue, Move For Hunger is harnessing the power of relocation. By partnering with moving companies worldwide, we collect non-perishable food items that would otherwise be discarded and donate them to local food banks. This simple yet impactful initiative transforms the act of moving into a catalyst for change, providing vital meals to those in need while reducing food waste.

Our Story

In 2009, a family preparing to move reached out to their moving company with a unique request. They had a pantry full of food they couldn't take with them and didn't want it to go to waste. The movers, inspired by the family's desire to help, decided to deliver the food to a local food bank. This simple act of kindness sparked the idea for Move For Hunger.

Since then, Sustain Share has grown into a nationwide movement. We partner with moving companies across the country to collect non-perishable food items from people who are moving and deliver them to food banks. Our mission is to reduce food waste and fight hunger by mobilizing the relocation industry to make a difference.

“If one company could make this kind of impact, what could an entire network of moving companies do?”

Empowering Families Through Education

Through our educational workshops, we empowered over 200 families with knowledge on sustainable farming practices. This led to improved crop yields and a more self-sufficient community, proving that education is a key driver for change.

Feeding 10,000 in Six Months

"In just six months, our community food drive managed to distribute over 10,000 meals to families in need. Volunteers from all walks of life came together, demonstrating the power of collective action and kindness."

Recycling for a Greener Future

"After partnering with local farmers, we transformed surplus produce into nutritious meals for underprivileged children. This initiative not only reduced food waste but also provided healthy food options to over 1,500 kids in our area.

WHY WE FIGHT

Every day, millions of people around the world go to bed hungry. Food insecurity is a global crisis that affects not only individuals but entire communities. At Sustain Share, we believe that no one should have to choose between food and other basic needs. Our mission is to fight hunger by ensuring that surplus food is redistributed to those who need it most.

Hunger is not just a lack of food; it’s a barrier to education, employment, and a better future. We fight to break this cycle by mobilizing communities, raising awareness, and advocating for sustainable solutions. Our efforts focus on long-term impact, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to thrive without the fear of hunger.

"Small acts, when multiplied by millions of people, can transform the world."
- Greta Thunberg
