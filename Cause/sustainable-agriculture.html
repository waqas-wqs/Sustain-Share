Sustainable agriculture balances food production with environmental protection, aiming to preserve resources for future generations. Unlike the post-war industrial model, it prioritizes ecosystem health, biodiversity, and social equity while ensuring economic viability.

Environmental Sustainability

Focuses on preserving natural resources like soil, water, and biodiversity.

Economic Viability

Ensures profitable and sustainable farming operations for farmers.

Social Equity

Prioritizes the well-being of farmers, workers, and communities.
